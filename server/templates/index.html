• Lip pop
    • A popping noise made w/lips, somewhat similar to a waterdrop noise
    • Purse your lips lightly. Quickly, but with minimal force, simultaneously blow a minuscule bit of air out between the center of your lips and part your lips at the same time. The small amount of air pressure will help w/the parting of your lips. Your mouth only needs to open a small amount. Although not necessary, it helps if your lips are slighty moist

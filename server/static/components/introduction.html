Contributing

There are a large number of phonetically-diverse sentences ready to record, taken from the TIMIT prompts.

  1. Pick a sentence to record.
  2. When you are ready to record the sentence, press the round Record button.
  3. After pressing Record, wait for about one second, then read the sentence out loud in a clear voice.
  4. When you are done speaking, wait a moment, then press the square Stop button. (If you don't wait, your mouse click can end up in the recording).

  5. You can then select another sound from the list and repeat the process.
  6. These sounds will be used to train a speech recognition neural network. More recordings as well as different voices, microphones, and environments will help the training.
  7. Keyboard shortcuts: You can use "space" on your keyboard to record/stop, and the arrow keys to advance the list.

If you have questions or comments, join the Talon Slack and ask in the #speech-dataset channel.
